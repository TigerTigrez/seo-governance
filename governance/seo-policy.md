# SEO Policy (Public Template)
**Scope:** Web properties under ORGANIZATION_NAME.  
**Principles:** Security-by-default, least privilege, accessibility-first.

## 1. Change Control
- All SEO-impacting changes require a PRD and ticket (see /templates/prd-seo-change.md).
- Deploys follow the Change Advisory cadence with rollback plans attached.

## 2. Privacy & PII
- No raw user-level data in SEO artifacts.
- Redaction SOP: see /governance/pii-redaction-SOP.md.

## 3. Accessibility
- SEO recommendations cannot reduce WCAG 2.2 AA conformance.
- See /governance/accessibility-wcag-checklist.md.

## 4. Auditing & Evidence
- Each recommendation must include: hypothesis → metric → expected impact → validation plan.
